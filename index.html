    How to Use the Floorplan Designer
    1. 1 Drag and drop furniture from the inventory to the floor plan area.
    2. 2 Move items by dragging. Right-click & drag to rotate.
    3. 3 Double-click any placed item to remove it.

    5. 5 Save, load, or download your design as a PDF.
